Meet Heath Shields

Heath Shields is a Republican candidate for South Dakota State Treasurer, bringing a lifetime of South Dakota values and common-sense business experience to the race. Raised with a deep appreciation for hard work and fiscal discipline, Heath understands the importance of managing money responsibly—because that's what South Dakota families do every single day.

As an entrepreneur and businessman, Heath has owned and operated multi-million-dollar businesses, balanced budgets, made payroll, and navigated complex financial landscapes. He's not a career politician; he's a problem-solver who believes government should be transparent, accountable, and efficient. Heath is running for State Treasurer to be a watchdog for your tax dollars, ensuring every cent is protected and invested wisely. He is committed to shielding our state's finances from wasteful spending and bureaucratic overreach.

Seven Core Priorities

Return Unclaimed Property Faster

Streamlined systems so every South Dakotan can easily find and claim what belongs to them.

Enhance Financial Transparency

Open, searchable reporting so taxpayers see where every dollar goes.

Strengthen Fiscal Security

Disciplined management to protect the state’s financial systems.

Modernize Processes & Technology

Upgrade outdated workflows to cut waste and deliver better service.

Improve Citizen Communication

Make the office accessible and responsive for families and small businesses.

Promote Financial Education

Encourage responsible stewardship at home, in business, and across government.

Protect the Permanent Trust Funds

Safeguard long-term investments so future generations inherit a strong South Dakota.

Seven Commitments to South Dakotans
